About Ali Mehdi

I hold a Master's in Computer Science with specialization in AI, and apply my expertise as Senior Director at Heritage Lab and CEO of Pytri. My work spans technology development and critical artistic exploration, with a focus on Indigenous language preservation, ethical AI, and cultural representation in technology.

  • CEO at Pytri, building bacteria and cancer quantification technology using AI
  • Senior Director at Heritage Lab, an Indigenous-led non-profit building community-based language models
  • Created VR drone filming techniques to explore birds-eye perspective experiences
  • Developed manga generators that properly represent Inuit facial features
  • Currently building robotic animals that express environmental conditions through interaction with AI

My approach balances technological innovation with critical perspectives on big tech, exploring the promises and perils of AI through both practical applications and artistic expression.
